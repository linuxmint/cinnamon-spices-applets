### Description

Azan is an Islamic prayer times applet for Cinnamon.

### Features

- List the 5 prayer times and other necessary times in Islam (Imsak, sunrise, sunset & midnight).
- Show the remaining time for the upcoming prayer.
- Show the current date in Hijri calendar.
- Get a reminder when it's time for a prayer, or before a prayer by 5, 10 or 15 minutes.
- Supports various calculation methods, madhhabs and time formats.
- Manually increase or decrease times up to 60 minutes for further fine tuning.

### Installation

1. Download the zip file and extract to `~/.local/share/cinnamon/applets`, or open "System Settings" > "Applets" > "Download", search "Azan" then click "Install" (the down arrow icon).
2. In "System Settings" > "Applets" > "Manage", select "Azan" the click "Add" (the plus icon).
3. For configuration, click on the applet on your panel and click "Azan Settings", or right click the applet on your panel and click "Configure", or in "System Settings" > "Applets" > "Manage", select "Azan" and click on "Configure" (the gear icon).
